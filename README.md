# Privacy Policy for Qibla Compass

Last updated: March 2026

Qibla Compass respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information

Our application may access your device's location to determine the direction of the Qibla accurately. This location data is used only for calculating the Qibla direction and is not stored or shared.

### Device Information

We may collect limited device information such as device model, operating system version, and advertising identifiers to improve app performance and display advertisements.

### Advertising

This application uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect information used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Learn more about how Google uses data:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

• Improve application performance
